# UX Documentation and AI Survey 📚🤖

This repository will contain materials and outputs for an exploratory survey study on **UX research documentation**, **traceability needs**, **AI-supported documentation practices**, and **human oversight**.

The study investigates how UX and user research practitioners document research work, how they connect empirical material to findings and design or product decisions, and what role generative AI may play in future documentation workflows.

## Study Overview 🔍

The survey focuses on three main questions:

1. **Current documentation practices**  
   What information, tools, and organizational practices characterize current UX research documentation?

2. **Traceability needs**  
   Which documentation problems and traceability needs do respondents report?

3. **AI support and human oversight**  
   How do respondents use AI for documentation, and what forms of checking, provenance, and human control do they expect?

## What Will Be Included 📦

This repository is currently being prepared. The planned release includes:

- anonymized survey data,
- the survey questionnaire,
- the Jupyter notebook used for descriptive analysis,
- generated figures used in the paper and appendix,
- generated tables and supplementary descriptive outputs,
- notes on data cleaning, incomplete responses, and per-question denominators,
- the Python requirements file for reproducing the analysis.
